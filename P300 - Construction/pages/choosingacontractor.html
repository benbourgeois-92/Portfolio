Choosing a Contractor

As technology rapidly changes so does the design specifications and state building codes for new construction and construction repairs & modifications. This guide is written to assist you on what to look for when planning for the need of a contractor. Often customers are confused with construction terminology used by contractors or salesmen when discussing their interest in their services. This guide seeks to ensure that you are talking the same language and comparing apples to apples when comparing construction specifications, laws and building codes with a contractor.
